About

Recimy is an app built with Ruby on Rails that enables users to create their own online cook-book. Once a recipe is uploaded, other users can check it out via the archive or through a category search. See a recipe that looks good? Add it to your cook-book! If you don't see a category that applies to your recipe, feel free to create one that does.
